The Marathon: More Than a Sport

Introduction

  • Recommendation: The Marathon
  • Significance
    • Ultimate testament to human spirit
    • Challenge limits
    • Never compromise

Sportsmanship (Key Point 1)

  • Icon: Eliud Kipchoge
    • Philosopher-athlete
  • Motto: “No human is limited”
  • Impact
    • Transforms physical test -> Symbol of possibility
    • Inspires students to break barriers

Public Fitness (Key Point 2)

  • Accessibility
    • Low barrier (Just shoes)
    • Crucial for students
  • Ripple Effect
    • Combats sedentary lifestyle
    • Promotes physical activity
  • Community
    • Example: Beijing Marathon
    • Proposal: Campus Mini-Marathon (Cultural exchange)

Conclusion

  • Basic Desires
    • Achievement
    • Connection
    • Well-being
  • Closing Thought
    • Finish line is within reach
    • Courage to pursue
